#  Barklet
![Legend of the Primal Isles/Images/Wildkins/Barklet.png\|300](/img/user/Legend%20of%20the%20Primal%20Isles/Images/Wildkins/Barklet.png) 

Sprouted from the raw earth, these sturdy wildkin have bodies built from ironclad bark. As they mature, their bodies grow more lush with vines, leaves, and flowers. Among their kind, a bountiful display of vegetation is a mark of honor

*Small beast, shares trainer alignment*
*Evolution: 1/3 - Rarity: Uncommon*

##### Powers
**Spellcasting.** Barklet knows the following spells, which are cast innately. 
Spell attack roll +5, save DC 13. 
- Cantrips: [[Legend of the Primal Isles/DND SRD/Spells/Poison Spray\|Poison Spray]] 
- 1st level (1/day): [[Legend of the Primal Isles/DND SRD/Spells/Entangle\|Entangle]]
##### Actions
**Vine Slap.** Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 10 ft. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 2) slashing damage.

**Underground Ambush (1 CP).** Barklet must have emerged from burrowing within 15 ft. of an enemy creature. Barklet makes a Vine Slap attack with Advantage. On a success, the target must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw. If the creature fails, they are pulled headfirst 15 ft. into a crater created by Barklet’s burrowing. The target is Restrained (escape DC 13) and Blinded  while Restrained in this way, until they escape or an ally uses their action to pull the stuck target free, requiring a DC 13 Strength (Athletics) check.
